Welcome to ProTrack, your straightforward, guided resource designed for absolute beginners entering the world of programming and technology. We believe the biggest barrier to learning to code is knowing where to start.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide clear, structured pathways (or "tracks") that guide you from zero experience to a foundational understanding of key programming domains, like Web Development or Data Science. We eliminate decision fatigue by curating the best free, external resources (like W3Schools, freeCodeCamp, etc.) into an easy-to-follow curriculum.

How ProTrack Works

We break down complex skills into achievable "Phases." Our beginner tracks focus on Phase 1: Foundations. When you select a track, you get a clear overview of the skills you will master and are immediately linked to the best resource to begin learning that specific topic. We don't host the courses; we host the map!

Key Features

  • Curated Learning: We hand pick top quality, free materials so you don't waste time searching.
  • Focused Paths: Our tracks are hyper focused on foundational skills necessary for your chosen career path.
  • No Enrollment Hassle: Start instantly no payment, no endless sign-up forms, just learning.
